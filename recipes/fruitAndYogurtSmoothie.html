Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons white sugars
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

Steps

  1. Step 1:Combine strawberries, banana, yogurt, pineapple juice, sugar, orange juice, and milk in a blender; blend until smooth.
